Alzheimer's detection using MRI scan

About

Alzheimer’s disease is a progressive neurodegenerative disorder that primarily affects memory, thinking, and behavior. It is the most common cause of dementia and leads to a gradual decline in cognitive abilities, impacting daily life and independence. While there is no known cure, early detection and intervention can play a crucial role in managing symptoms and improving patient care.
Our project focuses on leveraging MRI scans for the early and accurate detection of Alzheimer’s disease. Through advanced imaging techniques and AI-driven analysis, we aim to identify key biomarkers associated with neurodegeneration, helping healthcare professionals make informed diagnostic decisions. Detecting Alzheimer's in its early stages enables timely interventions, allowing patients and caregivers to explore treatment options, lifestyle modifications, and support systems that can enhance quality of life.
By combining cutting-edge technology with medical research, we strive to contribute to the global effort in combating Alzheimer’s disease—empowering doctors with precision-driven tools and offering hope for those affected.
